Marshall is a Masters student in Mathematics at the University of Manitoba, which is also where he obtained his BSc (Hons) in Math. His research interests mostly lie within Discrete Math, with a particular passion for Graph Theory. He loves teaching math and spends much of his time working with students in various contexts and at various levels, including TAing & running workshops, olympiad training, and Kumon. He's always eager to take on new projects, with recent additions being a guest-editor position at Crux Mathematicorum, volunteering for high school workshops, and doing math outreach programs such as Math Mania.

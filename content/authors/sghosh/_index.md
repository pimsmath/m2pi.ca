I am a postdoctoral fellow at the University of Calgary, with a PhD in Mathematics from the University of Toronto. My academic research background is in number theory, especially analytic and probabilistic number theory, and applications to cryptography and cybersecurity. I specialize in approaching complex problems with mathematical precision, structural thinking, and careful quantitative analysis. I am interested in applying this background in industry settings involving cryptography, cybersecurity, data science, and quantitative modelling, particularly where rigorous mathematical thinking can support secure systems, data-driven decision making, and complex analytical problems.
